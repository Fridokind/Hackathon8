University of Buea Computer Science Students Association

UBCOMSA is a community for all current and former student of computer science department of the university of Buea.

WELCOME TO UB/COMSA

As a non-profit organisation, UBCOMSA has for aim bring together current student as mentees, and former students as mentors in all the fields of computer science.

At UBCOMSA the main activity is bridging the gap between theory and practical in the field of computer science. At UBCOMSA you can fiend mentors in backend web development, frontend web development, Artificial intelligence, data science, software engineering, hardware development and much more.
